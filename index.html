Tic Tac Toe

Just a basic Tic Tac Toe game against the computer.
Once the game is over, either from the player's win, computer's win or from a tie, it'll be written down.
You can reset the game once it's over by clicking on the reset button.
There's also a turn counter, because why not ?
Finally, this little homework was the occasion for me to work a bit on design 'cause I love pretty things, so I've worked on neomorphism as well as neon effects.
